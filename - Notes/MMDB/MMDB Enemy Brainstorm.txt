
12 ideas per stage

Police Stage
	- Helicopter flies on top and shoots down at player
	- Shotgun police officer (shoots in 3 directions at player)
	- Police car drives by and shoots at player
	- Robber (takes items and walk/fall/jump)
Remote Stage
	- Sine wave, horizontal homming (satellite)
	- Shot circles around enemy
	- Boomerang shooter (standing, may aim to hit on go or back)
	- Dash towards player and shoot in 4 directions (alternate between orthogonal and diagonal?)
Jungle Stage
	- Monkey jumps from tree to tree and throws fruit (limited ammo in each tree). Or shoot 3 times and jump down on player instead?
	- Gravity shooter (seed turns into plant trap)
Gas Stage
	- Drills/Bombs destroy tiles
	- Chase player slowly, explode when destroyed/hit wall, explosion shoots in 8 directions
	- Go straight horizontally, flamethrower up and down
Ocean Stage
	- Homing fish (acceleration homing)
	- Shoot in 6 directions (alternate between 2 patterns - include vertical; include horizontal)
	- Come straight from front/back
Vacuum Stage
	- Spinning blade shooter (bounce at 90 degree)
	- Shoot 3 gravity shots (3 distances)
Ghost Stage
	- Teleport behind player and attack melee / short shot
	- Teleport to random location and shoot at player
	- Pumpkins from the background fly up then dash towards player and explode
Chain Stage
	- Swing (enemy swing ball / enemy itself is swinging)
	- 3 dashes in player direction, pause (telegraph, 8 direction rounding)
	- Bulb Jumper
